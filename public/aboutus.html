Meet Our Team

Prof. Dr. David S. Johnson

Prof. Johnson is a faculty member at CITEC, Bielefeld University, focusing on human cognition, collaborative interaction, and computational explanations. As the principal investigator, he oversees the project design, supervises development, and provides conceptual guidance, particularly on integrating the BLOCKIES framework with hypothesis-driven diagnosis.

Summaya Ashfaqs

Summaya is a PhD student working on explainable AI and human-AI collaboration. She contributes to the methodological design and user study evaluation, helping define how hypotheses and explanations are structured within the interface to support effective decision-making.

Lokesh Mangamuri

Lokesh is a Master's student in Interactive Intelligent Systems. He develops the full-stack web application for the project, focusing on building a user-friendly diagnostic tool with AI integration, database storage, and session management to enable smooth experimental workflows.

Karthikeya Barla

Karthikeya is involved in developing the XAI explanation components. He works on generating interpretable feedback and explanation visuals that help users understand AI support in decision-making scenarios within the interface.
